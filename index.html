about me

i'm at the university of illinois urbana-champaign majoring in computer science + linguistics and minoring in math. i'm interested in ai,nlp, programming language theory, and systems software. specifically, i have done research in the past on nlp alongside dr. elisa ferracane. i'm also an ardent linux user and i currently daily drive fedora arch linux on my framework laptop (although the more i look at it, the more i get enthralled with the idea of nixos). currently, i'm the treasurer for ACM@UIUC - the largest collective of CS students at Illinois - where i manage funds and enable many students across the club to pursue their passions and build projects.

outside of academics, i was a policy debate competitor in high school, and i am continuing this activity in college by coaching a group of highly motivated high schoolers from small programs, similar to the one i was from. i also enjoy playing many old Nintendo games and enjoy being part of the homebrew scene for many of these older consoles. a not so long time ago, i went to fox chapel area high school.
